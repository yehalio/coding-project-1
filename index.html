Why do I want to learn code?

I love to solve problems and create projects. Also, I love to take new approaches to problems. I feel like coding encapsulates these concepts and more. Coding is a skill I want to learn as it would enable me to work on projects for myself and for the benefit of others, develop new skills, and would allow me to create something or sell a service (I'm an entrepreneur at heart).
